# Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO)
**Masar Digital Bank | Baghdad, Iraq**

---

## About Masar Digital Bank

Masar Digital Bank is Iraq's first regulated digital bank, working toward an operating licence from the Central Bank of Iraq (CBI). Our mission is to bring modern, accessible banking to underbanked Iraqis through a mobile-first platform offering personal accounts, P2P transfers, card issuance, and bill payments.

---

## Role Overview

The MLRO is the first and most critical regulatory hire — required by the CBI before go-live. The MLRO is the nominated officer responsible for all AML, CTF, and financial crime compliance obligations and reports directly to the Board.

---

## Responsibilities

- Serve as the designated MLRO under CBI AML/CTF regulations and applicable Iraqi financial crime legislation
- Develop, implement, and maintain the Bank's AML/CTF policies, procedures, and controls
- Oversee compliance with CBI AML directives, FATF recommendations, and GoAML reporting requirements
- Review and approve Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs); submit to the Iraqi financial intelligence unit
- Design and deliver AML/CTF training programmes for all staff
- Conduct regular risk assessments of products, customers, and channels
- Lead the KYC/CDD/EDD framework in coordination with technology and operations teams
- Liaise with the CBI and other regulatory bodies on AML/CTF matters
- Advise the Board on emerging financial crime risks and regulatory developments
- Oversee transaction monitoring systems and alert management
- Manage regulatory examinations and audits relating to financial crime compliance

---

## Requirements

- Minimum 7 years of AML/CTF compliance experience within a bank, digital bank, or regulated financial institution
- Deep knowledge of CBI AML regulations, Iraqi banking law, and FATF standards
- Demonstrated experience filing SARs and engaging with financial intelligence authorities
- Experience designing AML frameworks from the ground up (greenfield bank experience preferred)
- Strong understanding of digital banking products, KYC technology (biometric ID, liveness checks), and transaction monitoring platforms
- ICA or ACAMS certification strongly preferred
- Prior experience working with or presenting to a central bank regulator
- Familiarity with GoAML schema and reporting
- Fluency in Arabic and English required
- Based in or willing to relocate to Baghdad, Iraq

## Desirable

- Experience with card scheme compliance requirements (Visa/Mastercard)
- Previous MLRO designation at a licensed Iraqi or regional bank

---

## Reporting Line

- **Reports to:** Board of Directors / CEO
- **Works closely with:** CFO, Head of Compliance, Legal Counsel, CBI relationship lead
