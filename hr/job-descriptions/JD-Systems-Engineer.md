# Systems Engineer
**Masar Digital Bank | Baghdad, Iraq**

---

## About Masar Digital Bank

Masar Digital Bank is Iraq's first regulated digital bank, working toward an operating licence from the Central Bank of Iraq (CBI). Our mission is to bring modern, accessible banking to underbanked Iraqis through a mobile-first platform offering personal accounts, P2P transfers, card issuance, and bill payments.

---

## Role Overview

Responsible for the integration and reliability of Masar's core banking systems, vendor APIs, and CBI connectivity. This role bridges infrastructure and software — owning the integrations between the Bank's platform and critical external systems including Progressoft (core banking switch), Nymcard (card issuing), Uqudo (KYC), and CBI (RTGS/Switch).

---

## Responsibilities

- Own the integration architecture between Masar's platform and all third-party vendors (Progressoft, Nymcard, Uqudo, SMS OTP, WhatsApp Business API)
- Design and implement CBI connectivity: RTGS integration (via intermediary initially, then direct), Switch, GoAML transaction reporting
- Manage API onboarding and sandbox testing for all vendors once contracts are signed
- Build and maintain integration test suites covering the full transaction lifecycle: KYC → account open → P2P transfer → card transaction → statement
- Define and implement system reliability standards: SLAs, health checks, circuit breakers, retry logic
- Document all system integrations, data flows, and interface specifications for CBI regulatory review
- Support incident management for system-level failures affecting core banking or vendor connectivity
- Contribute to ISO 27001 and CBI-mandated technical standards documentation
- Work with Algbra on backend service architecture and API contract definitions

---

## Requirements

- Minimum 5 years of systems or integration engineering experience in a banking or payments context
- Experience integrating with core banking switches (Progressoft CBS or equivalent strongly preferred)
- Understanding of card processing systems and card scheme connectivity (Visa/Mastercard BIN, ISO 8583)
- Experience with CBI or equivalent central bank connectivity (RTGS, SWIFT, national payment switches)
- Strong API integration skills (REST, ISO 20022, ISO 8583)
- Experience with KYC/identity verification API integration (biometric ID, liveness detection)
- Systems documentation experience for regulatory review
- Based in or willing to relocate to Baghdad, Iraq

## Desirable

- Direct experience with Progressoft, Nymcard, or Uqudo products
- Familiarity with GoAML transaction reporting schema
- Experience in a greenfield or challenger bank build
- Knowledge of Iraqi banking system architecture and CBI technical standards
- Arabic language skills strongly preferred
