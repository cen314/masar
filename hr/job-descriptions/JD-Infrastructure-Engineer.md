# Infrastructure Engineer
**Masar Digital Bank | Baghdad, Iraq**

---

## About Masar Digital Bank

Masar Digital Bank is Iraq's first regulated digital bank, working toward an operating licence from the Central Bank of Iraq (CBI). Our mission is to bring modern, accessible banking to underbanked Iraqis through a mobile-first platform offering personal accounts, P2P transfers, card issuance, and bill payments.

---

## Role Overview

Founding technical hire responsible for designing, building, and operating the physical and virtual compute infrastructure powering Masar Digital Bank. Works with technology leadership and external vendor partners to deliver a CBI-compliant, resilient banking infrastructure stack.

---

## Responsibilities

- Design and build the Bank's infrastructure stack: physical hardware → virtual compute → platform software → Kubernetes
- Procure, rack, configure, and maintain physical servers (Dell/HP) at the Baghdad data centre and co-location facility
- Deploy and manage Google Distributed Cloud Virtual (GDCV) or equivalent on-premise Kubernetes platform
- Configure and maintain network infrastructure: layer 1 switches, load balancers, firewalls
- Integrate with ATM and kiosk connectivity infrastructure
- Establish secure connectivity to CBI systems (RTGS, Switch) and external vendors (Progressoft, Nymcard, Uqudo)
- Implement disaster recovery (DR) and business continuity (BCP) procedures
- Maintain infrastructure security controls: network segmentation, access control, encryption at rest and in transit
- Contribute to ISO 27001 and other CBI-mandated standards
- Monitor infrastructure performance and capacity; respond to incidents

---

## Requirements

- Minimum 5 years of infrastructure engineering experience in banking, fintech, or enterprise environments
- Hands-on experience with on-premise server procurement, installation, and configuration (Dell PowerEdge or HP ProLiant preferred)
- Experience with Kubernetes on-premise (GDCV, OpenShift, or Rancher a strong plus)
- Solid networking fundamentals: VLANs, BGP, firewalls, load balancing
- Experience with data centre operations, co-location, and physical security standards
- Familiarity with banking infrastructure: HSMs, secure key management, CBI connectivity protocols
- Strong Linux systems administration skills
- Based in or willing to relocate to Baghdad, Iraq

## Desirable

- Knowledge of ATM and kiosk connectivity (ISO 8583 or NDC)
- ISO 27001 or equivalent security certification
- Experience in regulated financial environments in the MENA region
- Arabic language skills
