# DevOps Engineer
**Masar Digital Bank | Baghdad, Iraq**

---

## About Masar Digital Bank

Masar Digital Bank is Iraq's first regulated digital bank, working toward an operating licence from the Central Bank of Iraq (CBI). Our mission is to bring modern, accessible banking to underbanked Iraqis through a mobile-first platform offering personal accounts, P2P transfers, card issuance, and bill payments.

---

## Role Overview

Founding hire who will build and own the Bank's software delivery pipeline, container orchestration, and operational tooling. Will establish CI/CD, release management, and monitoring practices from the ground up — working closely with the mobile app team, Algbra (external tech partner), and the Infrastructure Engineer.

---

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement CI/CD pipelines for the Masar mobile app (iOS and Android) and backend services
- Build and manage container-based deployments on Kubernetes (on-premise GDCV or equivalent)
- Establish environment strategy: development, staging, production — with appropriate isolation and promotion gates
- Implement secrets management, certificate rotation, and secure configuration practices
- Set up application monitoring, alerting, and log aggregation (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK or equivalent)
- Manage release versioning and deployment automation for all Bank services
- Define and enforce infrastructure-as-code practices (Terraform or equivalent)
- Support incident response: on-call rotation, runbooks, post-mortems
- Collaborate with Infrastructure Engineer on Kubernetes cluster management, networking, and storage
- Contribute to security and compliance requirements (ISO 27001, SOC2, CBI IT standards)

---

## Requirements

- Minimum 4 years of DevOps or platform engineering experience, preferably in fintech or banking
- Strong experience with Kubernetes on-premise (GDCV, OpenShift, or Rancher)
- Hands-on CI/CD pipeline experience (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, or equivalent)
- Infrastructure-as-code experience (Terraform, Helm, Ansible)
- Solid Linux systems skills and shell scripting
- Experience with secrets management tools (HashiCorp Vault, AWS Secrets Manager, or equivalent)
- Monitoring and observability experience (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack)
- Based in or willing to relocate to Baghdad, Iraq

## Desirable

- Mobile app deployment experience (iOS App Store / Google Play release pipelines)
- Experience in regulated environments requiring audit trails and change management
- Knowledge of banking API security standards (mTLS, OAuth 2.0, API gateways)
- Familiarity with card processing infrastructure (Nymcard, Progressoft or similar)

---

## Reporting Line

- **Reports to:** CTO (when hired) / Technology Lead
- **Works closely with:** Infrastructure Engineer, Algbra, mobile app development team
