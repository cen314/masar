# Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
**Masar Digital Bank | Baghdad, Iraq**

---

## About Masar Digital Bank

Masar Digital Bank is Iraq's first regulated digital bank, working toward an operating licence from the Central Bank of Iraq (CBI). Our mission is to bring modern, accessible banking to underbanked Iraqis through a mobile-first platform offering personal accounts, P2P transfers, card issuance, and bill payments.

---

## Role Overview

The CFO is a founding leadership hire who will own all financial strategy, regulatory capital management, and financial operations. The role is critical to CBI licensing, investor reporting, and the Bank's path to profitability.

---

## Responsibilities

- Own the Bank's financial strategy, planning, and reporting from pre-launch through commercial operations
- Lead CBI capital adequacy submissions; ensure the Bank meets the $75M paid-up capital requirement and ongoing CBI prudential ratios
- Build and maintain the Bank's financial model including revenue forecasts, cost base, and 5-year P&L projections
- Establish financial controls, accounting policies, and internal audit frameworks compliant with CBI requirements and IFRS
- Manage relationships with external auditors, legal advisors, and the CBI on all financial matters
- Oversee treasury, liquidity management, and FX exposure
- Lead fundraising processes and investor reporting in coordination with shareholders
- Build and manage the finance team as the Bank scales
- Oversee budgeting, procurement, and vendor payment processes
- Ensure timely and accurate regulatory financial returns to the CBI

---

## Requirements

- Minimum 10 years of finance experience, with at least 5 years at CFO or Deputy CFO level within a bank or regulated financial institution
- Strong knowledge of CBI prudential requirements and Iraqi banking regulations
- Demonstrated experience managing regulatory capital submissions and central bank relationships
- Experience in a digital bank, challenger bank, or greenfield banking start-up strongly preferred
- Deep understanding of IFRS as applied to banking entities
- Track record of building financial models for regulated financial institutions
- CPA, ACA, ACCA, or equivalent professional qualification required
- Fluency in English required; Arabic a strong advantage
- Based in or willing to relocate to Baghdad, Iraq

## Desirable

- Experience working with the CBI or other MENA central banks
- Familiarity with core banking system financials (e.g. Temenos, Progressoft)
- Experience raising capital from institutional or sovereign fund investors
- UK/Iraqi dual background welcomed

---

## Reporting Line

- **Reports to:** CEO / Board of Directors
- **Works closely with:** MLRO, Legal Counsel, Algbra (tech partner), CBI
