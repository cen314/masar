# Compliance Officer — Regulatory & Technology (SOC2 / ISO)
**Masar Digital Bank | Baghdad, Iraq**

---

## About Masar Digital Bank

Masar Digital Bank is Iraq's first regulated digital bank, working toward an operating licence from the Central Bank of Iraq (CBI). Our mission is to bring modern, accessible banking to underbanked Iraqis through a mobile-first platform offering personal accounts, P2P transfers, card issuance, and bill payments.

---

## Role Overview

Will build and own Masar's regulatory compliance framework with a specific focus on technology compliance and information security certifications required by the CBI — including ISO 27001, SOC2, and any additional CBI-mandated standards. Works closely with the MLRO, CFO, and technology team.

> **Note:** Scope will be finalised in coordination with CBI requirements. The successful candidate may focus more heavily on IT/technology compliance or financial services compliance depending on the Bank's regulatory roadmap.

---

## Responsibilities

- Build Masar's compliance programme covering technology, operational, and financial services regulations
- Lead the ISO 27001 certification process — manage external ISO consultancy, define ISMS scope, oversee implementation
- Own the SOC2 readiness programme: gap assessment, control implementation, audit preparation
- Develop and maintain compliance policies, procedures, and controls across IT systems, data handling, and business operations
- Support the MLRO on AML/CTF controls that intersect with technology systems (transaction monitoring, KYC platform)
- Liaise with the CBI on technology and operational compliance requirements
- Manage vendor compliance assessments: ensure Progressoft, Nymcard, Uqudo, and other vendors meet the Bank's security and compliance standards
- Deliver staff compliance training across business and technology functions
- Maintain the compliance risk register and report to the Board on compliance posture
- Support external audits and regulatory examinations

---

## Requirements

- Minimum 5 years of compliance experience in banking, fintech, or a regulated financial institution
- Demonstrable experience leading or supporting ISO 27001 certification (managing external consultancies preferred)
- Understanding of SOC2 Type I/II audit requirements
- Knowledge of CBI regulatory requirements and Iraqi banking compliance obligations
- Experience with vendor due diligence and third-party risk management
- Strong documentation and policy writing skills
- Relevant qualification: ICA, CISI, CISM, CRISC, or equivalent
- Fluency in English required; Arabic a strong advantage
- Based in or willing to relocate to Baghdad, Iraq

## Desirable

- Prior experience at a digital bank achieving ISO certification from the ground up
- Familiarity with GDPR or data protection frameworks applicable to banking
- Experience working directly with a MENA central bank on compliance submissions
- Knowledge of card scheme compliance requirements (Visa/Mastercard rules)

---

## Reporting Line

- **Reports to:** MLRO / CEO
- **Works closely with:** CFO, Infrastructure Engineer, DevOps, all vendor compliance teams
